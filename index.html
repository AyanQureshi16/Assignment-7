About Toyota India

Toyota Kirloskar Motor Pvt Ltd (TKM) is a joint venture between Toyota Motor Corporation (Toyota), Japan's largest automobile company, and the Kirloskar Group, India's leading engineering conglomerate. Since 1997, we've been setting benchmarks in India's automotive industry.

With manufacturing plants in Bidadi, Karnataka, Toyota India produces a range of vehicles from premium sedans to powerful SUVs and MUVs, all built with Toyota's legendary quality and reliability.
